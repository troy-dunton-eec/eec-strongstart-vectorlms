Minimum Hiring Requirements for Educators

The Minimum Hiring Requirements for Educators support the goal of developing career pathways and opportunities for educators. EEC aims to support educators in pursuing meaningful qualifications and ongoing professional development to build their knowledge and skills working with children. We know well-prepared, well-supported educators are vital to ensure all children served in EEC-licensed programs receive high-quality education and care.
